## 🗓️ Itinerary
**7-Day Paris Trip Itinerary**

**Day 1: Arrival in Paris**

* Arrive at Paris Charles de Gaulle Airport (CDG)
* Take a taxi or metro (RER B) to your hotel
* Check-in at a budget-friendly hotel like Ibis Paris CDG Airport or Hôtel Mercure Paris Centre Tour Eiffel
* Spend the afternoon exploring the nearby area, such as the Montmartre neighborhood
* Enjoy a traditional French dinner at a local restaurant

**Day 2: Parisian Landmarks**

* Start the day with a visit to the iconic Eiffel Tower (stairs entrance: €7)
* Take a short walk to the nearby Champ de Mars park for a picnic lunch
* Visit the Arc de Triomphe (€12) and enjoy the stunning views of the city
* Explore the famous Champs-Élysées avenue and do some shopping
* Enjoy a Seine River cruise (€15) in the evening

**Day 3: Museums and Art**

* Visit the world-famous Louvre Museum (€18) and see the Mona Lisa
* Explore the Musée d'Orsay (€12) and its impressive collection of Impressionist art
* Visit the Montmartre neighborhood and see the beautiful Sacré-Cœur Basilica
* Enjoy a coffee at a charming café in the Latin Quarter

**Day 4: Royal Palaces**

* Visit the Palace of Versailles (€20) and its stunning gardens
* Take a day trip to the Fontainebleau Palace (€15) and its beautiful forest
* Enjoy a picnic lunch in the palace gardens
* Return to Paris in the evening and enjoy a dinner at a Michelin-starred restaurant

**Day 5: Markets and Food**

* Visit the famous Marché aux Puces de Saint-Ouen (flea market) and find some unique souvenirs
* Explore the historic Le Marais neighborhood and its trendy shops and cafes
* Visit the Galeries Lafayette (department store) and enjoy the stunning views of the city from its rooftop
* Enjoy a wine and cheese tasting (€30) in the evening

**Day 6: Parks and Gardens**

* Visit the beautiful Jardin du Luxembourg and its stunning gardens
* Explore the Champ de Mars park and its impressive views of the Eiffel Tower
* Visit the Parc des Buttes-Chaumont and its beautiful lake and gardens
* Enjoy a picnic lunch in one of the parks

**Day 7: Departure**

* Spend the morning shopping for last-minute souvenirs or visiting any last-minute attractions
* Take a taxi or metro (RER B) back to the airport for your departure flight
